Semantic Versioning

Interesting video, I never thought about what numbers meant when I looked at software and their version number. I had a general idea that the first number of a semantic version was always a big update. As for the other two numbers, I just assumed that they were meaningless and was only meant to show that there were some changes and that's it. Here (Links to an external site.)is one my favorite apps that utilizes semantic versioning. It's interesting to see that they use the traditional three number versioning until they release 5.0.14.0 and add an additional number, from my research they use the last number for "Additional labels for pre-release and build metadata are available as extensions to the MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH format". I'm still quite confused as to why to really need to add the last number.
